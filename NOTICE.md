## Bundled Sound Resources

Tickeys-Swift includes sound resources under `Resources/data`.

Known sound resource notices:

- `bubble`: sounds by Glaneur de sons from Freesound.org, licensed under Creative Commons Attribution 3.0. See `Resources/data/bubble/license.txt`.
- `drum`: sounds by Veiler from Freesound.org, licensed under Creative Commons 0. See `Resources/data/drum/_readme_and_license.txt`.
- `mechanical`: sounds by jim-ph from Freesound.org, licensed under Creative Commons 0. See `Resources/data/mechanical/license.txt`.

The following legacy Tickeys sound schemes are included for compatibility, but their original upstream attribution has not yet been fully confirmed in this repository:

- `Cherry_G80_3000`
- `Cherry_G80_3494`
- `sword`
- `typewriter`

If you own rights to any bundled sound resource or believe attribution is missing or incorrect, please contact the maintainer via:

```text
https://github.com/zjjfly/Tickeys-Swift/issues
```

The maintainer will review the request and update attribution, replace the resource, or remove the resource as appropriate.
